Chicken and Noodles

Description

This rich and filling Chicken & Noodles recipe is the perfect meal to warm up on a cold winter day! Here's what it takes:

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. boneless, skinless chicken breast, cubed
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp parsley, snipped
  • 2 Tbsp chicken bouillon granules
  • 1 tsp crushed dried thyme
  • .5 tsp pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 12 oz pkg. frozen egg noodles
  • 2 cups sliced carrots
  • 2 cups sliced celery
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Place chicken cubes, water, onion, celery leaves, parsley, bouillon, thyme, pepper, and bay leaf in a large Dutch oven.
  2. Bring to boiling.
  3. Add the noodles, carrots, sliced celery, & return to boiling.
  4. Simmer covered for 20 to 25 minutes or until noodles are almost tender.
  5. Stir in 1-1/2 cups milk and peas.
  6. Comgine 1/2 cup milk and flour until smooth. Stir into noodle mixture.
  7. Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly.
  8. Serves 8
