Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

It's not a party unless you have Buffalo Chicken Dip! Here's what it takes:

Ingredients

  • 4 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 1.5 cups cheddar cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients (but save a half cup of shredded cheese) into croc pot and stir together.
  2. Place lid on and set to LOW heat for 3-4 hours or HIGH for 1-2 hours. Stir every 30 minutes.
  3. The dip is ready when cheese is fully melted and you no longer see chunks of cream cheese.
  4. Sprinkle the remaining shredded cheddar cheese over the top and place the lid back on, letting the dip continue to cook until the cheese on top is melted.
  5. Serve with chips, crackers, or veggies.
