Chipotle Chicken

Description

The Chipotle Chicken is the perfect recipe to grill on a sunny, summer day. With a complex flavor pallette, the possibilities of pairings are endless! Here's what it takes:

Ingredients

  • 1 Lb Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chipotle chili powder
  • .5 tsp chili powder
  • .5 tsp ground cumin
  • .5 tsp smoked paprika
  • .5 tsp onion powder
  • .5 tsp garlic powder
  • .5 tsp salt
  • .25 tsp pepper
  • 1 Tbsp lime juice

Steps

  1. Mix all seasonings together in large bowl, adding chicken last
  2. Make sure chicken is covered by seasonings
  3. Allow to marinate refrigerated for at lease two hours
  4. Grill chicken on high heat until internal temp reaches 165. Perhaps 5 minutes per side.
